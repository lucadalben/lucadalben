Bricc
2021
Concept,
branding,
illustrations

Individual

Bricc is the project of a botanical bar for the restaurant Pierino Penati, located in the Brianza, in northern Italy. In order to involve a target of young people from 18 to 35 years old, we decided to convey the values of our project through the reference to the traditional dialect, bre.k (mountain hill) Bricc, communicating the values of territoriality and refinement. The aromatic herbs, peculiarities of the Montevecchia territory, characterize the cocktails with that personal and non-replicable touch, helping to make them unique.
