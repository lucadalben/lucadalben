Sportype is a web and mobile service that takes care of the decision-making phase of teenager and young people, helping users to choose the most suitable sport for them by providing a representation of sports and sports clubs free of gender bias. They can personally contact other users who have experienced difficulties in taking up a sport like them, so they can help and advise them to make the best choice. Through visual and textual language careful to avoid gender stereotypes, Sportype aims to communicate sport in a more inclusive way, without gender-based barriers.
