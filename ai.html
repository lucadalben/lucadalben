What is AI? How important is it: for us (as creatives), for the planet, and in general? Who should absolutely have an extensive say on this? That is what this experimental magazine is about. We first developed a deeper understanding about AI and the value of interviews. Then we created our own interview formats or deformed and adapted established formats. For example, the ChatGPT interview, the expert interview, the group chat, the interview without questions, the AI thought experiment, and many more. The goal: to generate new knowledge, real insights, and fresh content through careful, structured questioning and listening.
