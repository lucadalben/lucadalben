Mag Moda
2022
Concept, strategy, video making

Team

Andrea Molteni, Maria Nardi
Elisabetta Vignali, Davide Zoppi

Mag Moda is a white-label company that operates in the textile industry, designing and manufacturing women's apparel for other brands and sells directly to consumers with its own brand under the name "Luisella Mariani".
We were asked to analyze the company's market and business. Then, we developed a strategy to open a communication channel with the target audience. We designed the instagram page, with the goal of generating awareness, and the landing page, to create new leads and increase reputation. Finally, the video portrays the expertise and completeness of their service, made of people, gestures and attention to detail.
