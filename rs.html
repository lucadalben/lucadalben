Resew Journal
2022
Concept, research, layout, photography

Team

Giulia Zago, Andrea Benedetto,
Davide Saladino, Lucrezia Gestri

Resew Journal is a quarterly magazine on social welfare. It showcases the status quo, in which the welfare state, blinded by the rush to privatization and deregulation, is paradoxically fostering the expansion of this newly emerging class, the precariat. Focusing on OECD countries, each issue addresses a particular area of welfare policy intervention and presents two crucial adversities currently faced by many nations. For each, a series of paired articles proceeds through a narrative in which some of the main symptoms are highlighted, some of their consequences are raised, and, finally, some relevant measures, taken by the public or fiscal sector and the civil sector, are examined, highlighting how people are forming a strong, collective identity, filling gaps that the welfare state often fails to recognize.
