Concorsi Architetti Bologna
2021
Visual identity

Individual

On the occasion of the 10th anniversary of the platform "concorsiarchibo.eu" the Order of Architects of Bologna launched the call for the design of a logo that would represent it. The concept was born from the idea of enclosing in a single graphic sign the architecture symbol and the reference to the city of Bologna. The result is a pictogram of great simplicity and communicative effectiveness, which lends itself to be used and applied in different formats and on different media. For the realisation of its anniversary edition, the number 10 has been integrated, represented geometrically by exploiting the proportions between the elements.
